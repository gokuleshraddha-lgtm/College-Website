About Us

One of the Famous Engineering Colleges

Prof. Ramkrishna More College (Autonomous), established in 1992, is a prominent institution in the field of higher education located in Akurdi, Pune. Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), the college has consistently maintained a reputation for excellence in engineering education. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields, including B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), and Civil Engineering (CE), along with M.Tech programs in various specializations. The institution is recognized for its world-class infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a highly skilled faculty team. Prof. Ramkrishna More College has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for multiple programs, ensuring that the academic standards are on par with global best practices. The college has received Autonomous Status by UGC, enabling greater flexibility in curriculum design and implementation. It has been consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in Pune and Maharashtra due to its quality education, industry collaborations, and excellent placement records.
